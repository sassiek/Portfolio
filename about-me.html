About Me

Theologian turned actor turned coder...(interesting combination?) all whilst navigating the Cornish coast, wild swimming, and making macarons.

Last year I was studying Hindu feminism for my finals. This year I'm still studying - and still ferociously typing away in front of a screen - just this time it's in an entirely different language which is rather exciting (albeit somewhat alien at times). I've always been one for variety in life, studying, and never fitting into boxes - quite literally.

I graduated in 2020 from St John's College, Cambridge University where I studied Theology. Between translating Greek scripture and discussing female empowerment within Hindu communities you'd find me on the stage or walking the occasional fashion show (no tumbles to date...). (To be continued)
